Welcome, Traveler

My name is Konstantin, and I am glad you are taking a moment to bear witness to my journey.

My professional life has already led me down many different paths. I have been a carpet salesman, a landscape gardener, a temp in technical support for a software company, an office management professional, a construction worker, a railway safety lookout, a gatekeeper, and a logistics specialist. All of these stations have shaped me in one way or another, ultimately making me the person I am today. A patient seeker. A persistent wanderer and a reflective philosopher.

The new direction of my journey now leads me into the alluring darkness of Information Technology. This humble presence is meant to document this journey, growing with the knowledge I seek to acquire. And if you wish, dear guest, you are welcome to accompany me for a while and leave your own footprints in the sand.
